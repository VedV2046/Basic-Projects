The Hidden Chemistry of Daily Life
From your morning coffee to your nightly routine, chemicals are everywhere. Let's explore the science inside the items we use every day.
What Does Chemical Really Mean?

The word "chemical" can sound alarming, but it's important to remember that everything is made of chemicals—including water (H₂O) and the air we breathe (N₂, O₂). This page highlights some of the specific man-made and natural compounds in common products, explaining their purpose and why awareness is key.

Not Recommended Shampoos

Head & Shoulders (Anti-Dandruff variants)

Uses the harsh detergent SLES, which creates foam but strips natural oils from the scalp.

Contains Silicones that coat hair for a smooth feel but cause product build-up.

Uses strong synthetic fragrances that can be a source of scalp allergies.

Pantene (Pro-V variants)

Built on a harsh SLES base that strips hair and can lead to dryness and damage.

Loaded with Silicones to create a slippery feel, which prevents moisture from being absorbed.

Promotes a vicious cycle of stripping with sulfates, then coating with silicones.

Sunsilk (various variants)

Uses a harsh SLES base that can irritate the scalp and fade hair colour.

Coats hair with Silicones and polymers for an artificial feeling of thickness and shine.

Its signature scent comes from a powerful synthetic "Parfum" which can cause allergies.

Clinic Plus (Strong & Long Health)

Its simple formula is based on SLES, a very harsh and stripping detergent.

Focuses on aggressive cleansing rather than nourishing the hair or scalp.

Contains strong synthetic fragrances and other non-beneficial additives.

L'Oréal Paris (Total Repair 5)

Relies on SLES and other harsh sulfates despite its premium branding.

Creates a temporary "repair" effect by coating damaged hair with heavy Silicones.

Uses the classic sulfate-silicone combination for a cosmetic feel, not for true hair health.

Recommended Shampoos

Mamaearth (Onion Shampoo)

A sulfate-free shampoo that uses gentle, coconut-based cleansers which are gentle on skin.

Silicone-free, using natural oils and plant keratin to strengthen hair.

Free from parabens, mineral oils, and other toxins, making it safe for sensitive scalps.

Khadi Natural (Herbal Amla & Bhringraj Shampoo)

An Ayurvedic, sulfate-free shampoo that uses Reetha (Soapnut) for natural cleansing.

Contains herbs like Amla and Bhringraj to strengthen roots and promote growth.

A truly natural and therapeutic formula, free from silicones and parabens.

WOW Skin Science (Apple Cider Vinegar Shampoo)

A popular sulfate-free and silicone-free option with gentle, plant-derived cleansers.

Uses Apple Cider Vinegar to clarify scalp build-up and restore pH balance.

Free from parabens and mineral oils, focusing on natural shine and hair health.

Patanjali (Kesh Kanti Natural)

An affordable, sulfate-free shampoo that uses Reetha as a natural cleanser.

Formulated with Ayurvedic herbs like Bhringraj and Amla to nourish and strengthen hair.

A gentle choice for natural hair care that avoids silicones and other harsh additives.

Forest Essentials (Ayurvedic Shampoos)

A luxury, sulfate-free Ayurvedic shampoo that uses Reetha for gentle cleansing.

Contains plant extracts and essential oils for fragrance and real hair benefits.

Free from all petrochemicals, parabens, and silicones for a truly natural experience.
