The Hidden Chemistry of Daily Life
From your morning coffee to your nightly routine, chemicals are everywhere. Let's explore the science inside the items we use every day.
What Does Chemical Really Mean?

The word "chemical" can sound alarming, but it's important to remember that everything is made of chemicals—including water (H₂O) and the air we breathe (N₂, O₂). This page highlights some of the specific man-made and natural compounds in common products, explaining their purpose and why awareness is key.

Harmfull Products

Clean & Clear (Foaming Face Wash)

Uses the harsh detergents SLS/SLES, which aggressively strip the skin's natural moisture barrier.

Contains strong synthetic fragrances and colourants that can cause significant irritation and allergic reactions.

Promotes a "squeaky clean" feeling that is actually a sign of dryness, often leading to rebound oiliness and breakouts.

Neutrogena (Oil-Free Acne Wash)

Built on a harsh SLES base that can be excessively stripping, especially when combined with active ingredients.

The combination of a strong detergent and Salicylic Acid can cause severe dryness, redness, and peeling.

Contains artificial colours and a powerful fragrance, which are common and unnecessary skin irritants..

Himalaya (Purifying Neem Face Wash)

Despite its "herbal" marketing, its main cleansing agent is often a harsh sulfate like Ammonium Lauryl Sulfate or SLES.

Relies on a strong synthetic "Parfum" for its scent, which is a frequent trigger for contact dermatitis and skin sensitivity.

Focuses on an aggressive deep clean that can disrupt the skin's delicate pH balance and compromise its protective barrier.

Pond's (Pure Detox / White Beauty Face Wash)

Often uses Myristic Acid and Potassium Hydroxide high in the ingredients list, creating a high-pH, stripping foam.

Loaded with strong synthetic fragrances and often contains menthol for a "cooling" sensation that can be irritating.

Prioritizes a temporary cosmetic effect ("brightening" or "detox") over the long-term health and hydration of the skin.

Garnier (Skin Naturals variants)

Formulated with a harsh sulfate base to create the rich lather that consumers often associate with cleanliness.

Frequently contains denatured alcohol and potent synthetic fragrances, which lead to dryness and irritation.

The formula emphasizes aggressive cleansing and artificial scent rather than gentle, skin-supportive care.

Helpful Products

Cetaphil (Gentle Skin Cleanser)

A classic soap-free and non-foaming cleanser that is famously recommended by dermatologists for its mildness.

Fragrance-free and non-comedogenic, making it ideal for sensitive, dry, and acne-prone skin types.

Cleanses effectively without stripping the skin's natural protective oils, maintaining a healthy moisture barrier.

Simple (Kind to Skin Refreshing Facial Wash)

A 100% soap-free gel that uses gentle cleansers instead of harsh sulfates like SLS/SLES which help in nourishing the skin.

Contains no artificial perfumes, dyes, or harsh chemicals that can upset the skin's natural balance.

Enriched with skin-loving ingredients like Pro-Vitamin B5 and Vitamin E to soothe and nourish.

Mamaearth (Ubtan Face Wash)

A sulfate-free cleanser that uses gentle, coconut-based surfactants for a mild cleansing action.

Features natural ingredients like Turmeric and Saffron to brighten skin, and Walnut beads to gently exfoliate.

Free from parabens, silicones, and artificial fragrances, making it a safe choice for promoting natural skin health.

Plum (Green Tea Pore Cleansing Face Wash)

A soap-free and sulfate-free formula that is exceptionally gentle on the skin and helps in cleansing.

A vegan, cruelty-free choice that is free from parabens, SLS, and other common irritants.

Uses Green Tea extracts to combat acne and Glycolic Acid for gentle exfoliation of skin without harsh scrubbing.

Kama Ayurveda (Rose Jasmine Face Cleanser)

A luxury, sulfate-free Ayurvedic cleanser that uses natural, plant-based cleansing agents.

Formulated with pure Rose and Jasmine essential oils for a beautiful, natural fragrance without synthetic perfumes.

Contains soothing ingredients like Aloe Vera and Jojoba Seed oil to hydrate and nourish the skin while cleansing.
