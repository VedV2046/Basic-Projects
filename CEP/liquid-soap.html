The Hidden Chemistry of Daily Life
From your morning coffee to your nightly routine, chemicals are everywhere. Let's explore the science inside the items we use every day.
What Does Chemical Really Mean?

The word "chemical" can sound alarming, but it's important to remember that everything is made of chemicals—including water (H₂O) and the air we breathe (N₂, O₂). This page highlights some of the specific man-made and natural compounds in common products, explaining their purpose and why awareness is key.

Not Recommended Liquid Soap

Vim Liquid Dishwash Gel

Contains Sodium Laureth Sulfate (SLES), a harsh detergent that can strip the skin's protective moisture barrier.

Contains strong synthetic fragrances and artificial colours which serve no cleaning purpose but add to the chemical load.

Regular contact can lead to significant skin dryness, itching, and irritation, making it unsuitable for those with sensitive skin.

Pril Dishwashing Liquid

Uses SLES as its primary grease-cutting agent, a chemical known to irritate skin after repeated exposure.

Is scented with artificial "Parfum," that can legally hide dozens of chemicals, including potential allergens and hormone-disrupting phthalates.

The formula is designed for aggressive cleaning, which can strip the natural oils (sebum) from your hands, leaving them feeling tight and dry.

Exo Dish Shine Liquid

Has sulfate-based formula for its cleaning action, which is effective but can be harsh and irritating to the skin.

Gets its bright colour and strong, lingering smell from petroleum-derived synthetic dyes and perfumes, which lack the benefits of natural alternatives.

The overall risk comes from its reliance on multiple industrial chemicals, increasing the chances of skin irritation for the person washing the dishes

Patanjali Super Dishwash Gel

Surprisingly contains SLES as a key ingredient, which contradicts the natural, herbal image of the brand and can be an irritant.

Uses other synthetic additives and preservatives to achieve the desired foam, stability, and fragrance, making it chemically similar to other soaps.

This formulation can be misleading for consumers who specifically choose the brand expecting a product free from such common industrial detergents.

Giffy Dish Wash Gel

Formulated with the harsh surfactant SLES, which is effective on grease but is also a common trigger for contact dermatitis in susceptible individuals.

Contains bright artificial dyes (like Tartrazine or Sunset Yellow) which are purely cosmetic and can be a source of skin sensitivity and allergies.

The combination of a powerful detergent base with synthetic additives makes it a high-potential trigger for allergic reactions and skin irritation.

Recommended Liquid Soap

The Better Home Dishwashing Liquid

Made from 100% plant-based active ingredients, such as coconut- and sugarcane-derived surfactants, which are effective against grease but inherently gentle on the skin.

The entire formula is fully biodegradable, meaning its ingredients break down naturally and do not harm aquatic life or pollute water sources.

It is gentle enough for sensitive skin while being tough on grease without using any harsh chemicals like sulfates, phosphates, or parabens.

Born Good Plant-Based Dishwash Gel

Uses gentle coconut-based cleansers that effectively clean dishes without the risks associated with sulfates, making it ideal for families. Made from 100% plant-based active ingredients

Guarantees a certified toxin-free formula, explicitly stating it is free from SLS, SLES, parabens, phthalates, and artificial fragrances.

Cleverly includes natural grease-cutters like citric acid and vinegar, which are highly effective, food-safe, and environmentally friendly.

Koparo Natural Dishwashing Liquid

Features a pH-balanced, plant-powered formula that respects the natural pH of your skin, preventing the dryness and damage often caused by dishwashing.

Is scented with pure essential oils like lemongrass or lavender, which provide a natural, pleasant aroma without the health risks of synthetic perfumes.

Specifically designed to be non-drying and hypoallergenic, making it one of the best choices for people with very sensitive skin or eczema.

PureCult Natural Dishwashing Liquid

Effectively cleans dishes using non-toxic, plant-based surfactants derived from sources like corn and coconut, ensuring a safe cleaning experience.

The sophisticated aromas are derived directly from therapeutic-grade essential oils, such as geranium and lavender, which offer a calming scent experience.

As a brand, it is highly committed to being eco-friendly and non-toxic, ensuring the product is safe for your family, pets, and the planet.

Seventh Generation Dish Liquid

Made from 100% plant-based, biodegradable ingredients, reflecting the brand's long-standing to sustainability and environmental health.

Is completely free from unnecessary and potentially harmful additives like artificial dyes, synthetic fragrances, phosphates, and triclosan.

A globally trusted brand for environmental safety, its formula is EPA Safer Choice certified, guaranteeing it is safe for your family.
