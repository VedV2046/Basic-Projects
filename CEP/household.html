The Hidden Chemistry of Daily Life
From your morning coffee to your nightly routine, chemicals are everywhere. Let's explore the science inside the items we use every day.
GLASS CLEANER
Ingredients in Glass-Cleaner:

Water + Alcohol (ethanol/isopropanol) → quick drying

Ammonia (NH₃) → streak-free shine

Mild Surfactants → remove fingerprints, oil

Fragrance/Dyes
FLOOR CLEANER
Ingredients in Floor-Cleaner:

Water + Alcohol (ethanol/isopropanol) → quick drying

Surfactants → lift dirt & grease

Disinfectants → phenyl, bleach, quats (kill germs)

Fragrance & Color → pleasant smell

DEODRANT/PERFUME
Ingredients in Deodrants:

Alcohol → solvent, quick drying, kills germs

Antimicrobials (triclosan, alcohol) → stop odor bacteria

Aluminum salts → block sweat glands (antiperspirant)

Emollients (oils, waxes) → protect & soften skin

Fragrance → provides scent

Preservatives → extend shelf life
