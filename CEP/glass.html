The Hidden Chemistry of Daily Life
From your morning coffee to your nightly routine, chemicals are everywhere. Let's explore the science inside the items we use every day.
What Does Chemical Really Mean?

The word "chemical" can sound alarming, but it's important to remember that everything is made of chemicals—including water (H₂O) and the air we breathe (N₂, O₂). This page highlights some of the specific man-made and natural compounds in common products, explaining their purpose and why awareness is key.

Not Recommended Glass-Cleaners

Colin (Glass & Household Cleaner)

Relies on harsh solvents like Isopropyl Alcohol and other chemicals that can be strong respiratory irritants.

Contains potent synthetic fragrances and an artificial blue dye to create its signature look and smell, which can cause headaches.

Releases VOCs (Volatile Organic Compounds) that can compromise indoor air quality, making it unsafe for poorly ventilated spaces.

Windex (Original Blue)

Its primary active ingredient is Ammonia-D, a harsh chemical known to irritate the eyes, skin, nose, and throat.

The formula contains petroleum-derived solvents and surfactants that are not readily biodegradable.

Achieves its "streak-free" shine using a formulation that is harsh on the user and the environment.

Mr. Muscle (Glass Cleaner)

Uses a powerful combination of chemical solvents and detergents to aggressively cut through grime.

The strong chemical odour is often masked by an overpowering artificial fragrance, adding to the chemical load in your home.

Its conventional, non-biodegradable formula can be harmful if inhaled and leaves behind chemical residues.

CIF (Shine Glass Cleaner)

Uses a solvent-based formula with chemicals like Ethanolamine to cut through grease, which can be a skin and respiratory irritant.

Contains a strong, perfume-like synthetic fragrance that lingers and can be overwhelming in enclosed spaces.

Represents a typical chemical-heavy approach that prioritizes fast cleaning over user safety and environmental impact.

Generic / Store-Brand Cleaners

Often formulated with the cheapest available ammonia compounds and alcohols to keep costs low, resulting in harsh fumes.

Typically use very strong, generic artificial fragrances to mask the chemical smell, adding to the VOC load in the air.

Generally lack ingredient transparency and are designed to mimic the harsh, conventional formulas of leading brands.

Recommended Glass-Cleaners

DIY White Vinegar + Water Solution

A completely natural and effective cleaner using acetic acid (from vinegar) to easily dissolve dirt, grease, and mineral spots.

100% free from fragrances, dyes, and proprietary chemicals because you make it yourself (typically a 1:1 ratio of water to vinegar).

Extremely affordable, non-toxic, and biodegradable, making it perfectly safe for use around children and pets.

Herbal Strategi (Just Mop Glass Cleaner)

A plant-based formula that leverages natural ingredients and essential oils (like lemongrass) for its cleaning power.

Fully biodegradable and eco-friendly, ensuring no harmful chemical residues are left on surfaces or washed into the environment.

Free from ammonia, chlorine, and other toxins, making it a safe choice with a pleasant, natural fragrance.

Koparo Clean Natural Glass Cleaner

Employs plant-derived, non-toxic surfactants to break down dirt without relying on harsh, volatile chemicals to clean.

Completely free from ammonia, acids, and bleach, ensuring it is safe for your family and does not degrade indoor air quality.

A pet-safe, kid-safe, and environmentally conscious choice that is readily biodegradable and effective.

SOVI Glass & Surface Cleaner

Uses plant-based and mineral-derived ingredients and is certified cruelty-free, offering an effective clean without harsh chemicals.

The formula is readily biodegradable with a minimal impact on environmental life, making it an eco-conscious choice.

Free from ammonia, strong fragrances, and dyes, it provides a streak-free shine using the power of nature.

Seventh Generation (Free & Clear Glass Cleaner)

A plant-powered formula that is USDA Certified Biobased (96%), ensuring its ingredients are from renewable sources.

A non-toxic and biodegradable cleaner from a reputable brand that prioritizes ingredient transparency and sustainability.

Free from harsh solvents, ammonia, and artificial fragrances, making it hypoallergenic for sensitive individuals.
