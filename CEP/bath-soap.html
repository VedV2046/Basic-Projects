The Hidden Chemistry of Daily Life
From your morning coffee to your nightly routine, chemicals are everywhere. Let's explore the science inside the items we use every day.
What Does Chemical Really Mean?

The word "chemical" can sound alarming, but it's important to remember that everything is made of chemicals—including water (H₂O) and the air we breathe (N₂, O₂). This page highlights some of the specific man-made and natural compounds in common products, explaining their purpose and why awareness is key.

Not Recommended Bath Soaps

Lifebuoy

A classic synthetic detergent bar, not a true soap, that uses harsh surfactants which strip the skin of its natural, protective oils.

Its "advanced germ protection" relies on potent synthetic antibacterial agents that can disrupt the skin's healthy microbiome.

The formula is very drying and can lead to skin irritation and a feeling of tightness after a bath.

Dettol Soap

Marketed as an antiseptic bar, it uses strong synthetic antibacterial and antimicrobial chemicals that are harsh on the skin.

The high level of detergents and antiseptic agents can be extremely drying and may trigger skin conditions like eczema.

Its formula is designed to kill all germs, including the beneficial bacteria that are essential for healthy skin.

Lux

Relies on a powerful, complex synthetic "Parfum" for its signature scent, which can contain hidden allergens and phthalates.

The base is a standard syndet bar that cleans by stripping away natural oils, rather than gently cleansing.

Focuses on a sensory experience (fragrance and lather) rather than nourishing or protecting the skin's health.

Dove

Marketed as gentle, but it is a synthetic detergent bar, not a natural soap. Its main cleanser is Sodium Lauroyl Isethionate, not saponified oils.

While milder than other syndets, it still contains synthetic fragrances and preservatives to achieve its texture and scent.

It's a prime example of "hidden chemistry," as its "milder than soap" claim is based on it being a pH-balanced detergent, not a truly natural product.

Cinthol

A classic deodorant soap that uses a harsh detergent base to provide a "squeaky clean" feeling, which strips the skin.

Its iconic strong, "fresh" scent comes from a powerful synthetic fragrance compound.

The combination of its deodorant chemicals and stripping detergents can be very harsh and drying, especially for sensitive skin.

Recommended Bath Soaps

Mysore Sandal Soap

A classic example of a true soap, made using natural vegetable oils and pure, natural sandalwood oil.

It contains natural glycerin, which is a byproduct of the soap-making process and helps to moisturize the skin.

Free from harsh detergents and artificial additives, relying on the therapeutic and aromatic properties of sandalwood.

Himalaya Herbals

A true soap that avoids harsh chemicals like SLS and parabens in its formulation.

Uses natural oils combined with the powerful Ayurvedic herbs Neem and Turmeric for their natural antibacterial and anti-inflammatory benefits.

Cleans gently without stripping the skin, making it suitable for daily use even on sensitive or acne-prone skin.

Medimix Ayurvedic Soap

A famous Ayurvedic soap made with a unique formula of 18 different herbs and a coconut oil base.

It is a true, natural soap that was originally developed to treat skin ailments like acne, boils, and rashes.

Completely free from harsh detergents and synthetic additives, offering therapeutic benefits through its potent herbal ingredients.

Khadi Natural

These are quintessential handmade, natural soaps, created through the traditional saponification of oils like coconut and castor oil.

They retain the naturally occurring glycerin, making them highly moisturizing and non-drying.

Scented with natural essential oils and coloured with herbal powders, they are free from all synthetic chemicals.

Patanjali Soap

A widely available true sPatanjali Soapoap that uses a natural, vegetable oil-based formula.

Enriched with classic Indian skincare ingredients like Turmeric (Haldi), Sandalwood (Chandan), and Saffron (Kesar).

Offers a gentle, natural cleansing experience without the harshness of synthetic detergents, parabens, or artificial colourants.
