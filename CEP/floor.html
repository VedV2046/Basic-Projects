The Hidden Chemistry of Daily Life
From your morning coffee to your nightly routine, chemicals are everywhere. Let's explore the science inside the items we use every day.
What Does Chemical Really Mean?

The word "chemical" can sound alarming, but it's important to remember that everything is made of chemicals—including water (H₂O) and the air we breathe (N₂, O₂). This page highlights some of the specific man-made and natural compounds in common products, explaining their purpose and why awareness is key.

Not Recommended Floor Cleaners

Lizol (Disinfectant Surface Cleaner)

Uses Benzalkonium Chloride, a Quaternary Ammonium Compound (quat), which can be a potent skin and respiratory irritant.

Contains very strong synthetic fragrances and dyes to create a "clean" smell that can cause headaches and trigger allergies.

Leaves behind a chemical residue that can be harmful to toddlers and pets who have direct contact with the floor.

Domex (Disinfectant Floor Cleaner)

Formulated with Sodium Hypochlorite (bleach), a highly corrosive and harsh chemical that is aggressive on surfaces.

The fumes are a powerful irritant to the respiratory system and eyes; mixing it with other cleaners can create toxic gas.

Its formula is highly toxic to aquatic life when it enters waterways and is unsafe for use around pets.

Dettol (Liquid Disinfectant for Floor)

Relies on potent chemical disinfectants like Chloroxylenol which, while effective, can be harsh and irritating to the skin.

Has a signature strong "antiseptic" smell from a combination of chemicals and fragrance that can be overwhelming indoors.

Promotes a sterile environment using a harsh chemical approach that can be a concern for crawling babies and pets.

Harpic (Disinfectant Floor Cleaner)

Also uses Benzalkonium Chloride as its primary disinfectant, contributing to the chemical load and potential for irritation in the home.

Loaded with powerful artificial fragrances designed to linger for hours, which can be a source of constant indoor air pollution.

Its formula is petroleum-based, not readily biodegradable, and contributes to long-term water pollution.

Generic Phenolic Cleaners (White/Black Phenyl)

Contain Phenol or other phenolic compounds, which are caustic and toxic industrial chemicals even in dilution.

Have an extremely strong, pungent smell, and the fumes are known to be hazardous with prolonged exposure.

A very harsh and outdated option that is highly toxic if ingested by pets or children and is extremely damaging to the environment.

Recommended Floor-Cleaners

Herbal Strategi (Just Mop Herbal Floor Cleaner)

Uses a unique plant-based formula with natural disinfectants like Lemongrass, Pine, and Cedarwood oil.

Naturally repels insects without the use of harsh chemical pesticides, making it a dual-purpose and safe choice for homes.

A biodegradable, eco-friendly, and non-toxic cleaner that is explicitly safe for children and pets to be around.

Koparo Natural Disinfectant Floor Cleaner Liquid

Formulated with plant-derived, non-toxic surfactants and active ingredients for a gentle yet effective clean.

Contains no harsh chemicals like bleach, ammonia, or phosphates, ensuring better indoor air quality after mopping.

Biodegradable, pet-friendly, and baby-safe option that is certified by third parties to be free of common toxins.

Born Good (Plant-based Disinfectant Floor Cleaner)

Uses gentle coconut-based surfactants to lift dirt and germs from surfaces without harsh chemical action.

Scented with natural essential oils like Bergamot, Mandarin, and Brazilian Orange for a pleasant, non-toxic aroma.

A biodegradable, vegan, and cruelty-free formula that is safe for all kinds of sealed floors and all family members.

PureCult (Floor Cleaner powered by Geranium)

A soap-based cleaner made from natural oils that effectively cleans floors without leaving behind toxic residues.

Leverages the natural anti-bacterial properties and calming scents of Geranium and Lavender essential oils.

Free from phosphates, bleach, and artificial fragrances, this biodegradable cleaner is safe for the earth, pets, and people.

DIY Vinegar & Hot Water Solution

Uses the natural disinfecting and deodorizing power of acetic acid (from vinegar) to clean and shine floors.

Free of any synthetic chemicals, fragrances, or colours, making it one of the safest possible options.

An incredibly affordable, eco-friendly, and pet-safe solution that effectively removes grime without any toxic fumes.
